Food, substance consisting essentially of protain, carbohydrate, fat, and other nutrients used in the body of an organism to sustain growth and vital processes and to funish energy.
The absorbsion and utilization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrition and is facilitated by digestion.
Plant which convert solar energy to food by photosynthesis, are the primary food source.

Animal that feed on plants often serve as source of food for other animal.
